Project Stormwing — the beginning

project stormwing

On June 27th, we officially launched Project Stormwing. Project Stormwing is the first of its kind; "the premier African-led AI Cybersecurity Initiative" as a handsome guy once put it (me). With Project Stormwing, and seeing how several countries have been restricting access to AI Cybersecurity models, we aim to unite Africa into breaking the shackles that binds us onto unreliable and unobtainable resources. A sovereign Africa is a strong Africa, and that's what we aim to achieve.

With Project Stormwing — for Africa, by Africa — we aim to make our own proprietary AI defensive cybersecurity model, for Africans to protect their infrastructure — in Africa.

At the first time of asking, with an AI model that is far from ready, we've already been able to find an 8 year-old vulnerability in a python library that's in use in over 8000 open-source softwares worldwide.

Stay tuned for more, this is just the beginning. I will be narrating all the progress we've made all throughout this journey...
