ALTAAF'S ABSURDIST ADVENTURES

BEL vs SEN CDM 16eme

football

As much as Liverpool has given me the highest highs (*that* Wijnaldum header), I think it's at the World Cup that I get my lowest lows. Fucking Senegal maaann...

I'm at a point that I don't even want to watch tonight's matches... How did they let it get away like that?

Going into it I was so confident in ourselves—I wasn't even anxious. It was only Belgium. Belgium are shit right now. We're so much better than them. They can't handle our physicality. We'll bully them off the ball. Doku's gotta deal with Krepin Diatta's speed, Krepin's gonna eat him aliivve. Our will engulf KDB and Tieleman's, they'll come off the match with new found claustrophobia...

We were dominating... I even stopped actively watching after the first half. That's how much I knew we were winnning. They couldn't deal with our attack *or* defense. By the second goal I knew the rest of the match was just a formality.

...then we got complacent. We got arrogant. It felt like they wanted to humiliate Belgium. Showboat. Play with their food. Not even bothering going for the kill...

That goal was *so* preventable. Just kick it away? Why did you wanna get pretty with it? Did you *have* to pass around them? Rub that defeat in their noses? They lost the ball, Lukaku(?) scored, and from then on I knew it was over.

Belgium saw the equaliser in their sights. They were flying. We started panicking... We conceded, and even if we managed to keep it level until the penalties, I knew we were losing. My heart hurts. I feel sick. My stomach is so tight that it gets difficult to breathe.

Ah well... Morocco's through, and Egypt's still to play. Fingers crossed that they don't commit the same mistakes. I kinda indentify with Senegal though, I too tend to get overconfident sometimes (a lot) too lol.

In other news, la RDC played a hell of a match. Unfortunately, Harry Kane was just too good; especially that first goal, the second one was defendable if no4 marked him tighter. Yes, Mbemba was following him, but once it got into no4's zone, he should have gotten closer to him to prevent the shot, I don't know what he was trying to prevent staying away like that...
